Introduction

Its an E-Commerce Based Project, Shopping Bazar is been developed for helping out the peoples around the globe who wants an solution for selling their product online, AS this will be saving their money because many of the CMS like Shopify and other E-Com Based CMS are very expensive while in this kind of custom one will only need to buy it once.
We have create alot of features at the Admins End to make it more suitable for the people to perform the changes more easily, We've added reports generations modules for helping out the Sellers to findout how is it going. We added some Shopify's Features in it to make it more feasible for the Person coming from there .
We have Added an option to hire any manager for your work . We have report generation module which will be describing how much time a person is doing work in a particular session.
ShoppingBazar has all other user modules like wish list , Payment Gateways, Shop Pages, Product Description Pages. Many of the modules have sub features/modules in it for keeping the intrest of the buyer at the Shop.
